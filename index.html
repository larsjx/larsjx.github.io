We have a history of winning important lawsuits

Yung Keithly LLP's professionals are skilled trial lawyers with experience throughout Texas and many parts of the United States. Winning to our clients means successful results with minimal business disruption. Our attorneys have both a track record of winning and a commitment to the success of our clients.

Yung Keithly LLP concentrates on commercial litigation in a variety of practice areas. The firm currently handles lawsuits involving health care, business contracts, intellectual property, accounting malpractice, antitrust, fraud, investor disputes, and receiverships, among others. The firm has the capability to handle any business dispute or commercial litigation. Successful Results for Clients who are Plaintiffs or Defendants.

The firm was founded (originally named Scott Yung LLP from 2001 to 2012) when Andrew Yung and John Scott left the Dallas office of Susman Godfrey LLP. In 2012, the firm was renamed Yung Keithly LLP.

Our lawyers have won jury awards of over $30,000,000, and achieved over $200,000,000 in settlements for clients. Our attorneys have also earned 21 settlements on behalf of plaintiffs of $1,000,000, or more.

Our current clients include billion-dollar public and private corporations, state governments, investment professionals, private equity investors, and several of the nation's largest health plans. Clients trust us to battle their most formidable opponents. In the past decade, clients have hired our attorneys to litigate against the following multi-billion dollar companies: Merck, UnitedHealthcare, PricewaterhouseCoopers, Pfizer, Fujitsu, Abbott Laboratories, Southwestern Bell, and many others.
